## Ingredients  
- 2 medium sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed  
- 1 onion, sliced  
- 1 can (400 ml) coconut milk  
- 1 cup (240 ml) water or veggie broth  
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon  
- 1/4 tsp ground cardamom  
- Salt to taste  
- *Optional:* a pinch of chili flakes or curry powder for warmth  

---
