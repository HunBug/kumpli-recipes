### üçú **Classic Summer Soba (Zaru Soba)**

#### üßÇ Ingredients

##### For the noodles:

* 200g **dried soba noodles** (preferably 100% buckwheat, or at least 80/20 soba to wheat ratio)
* Ice and water (for chilling)

##### For the dipping sauce (*mentsuyu*):

* 1 cup **dashi stock** (kombu + bonito flakes preferred for full umami)
* 1/4 cup **soy sauce** (Koikuchi shoyu, the standard dark soy)
* 1/4 cup **mirin**
* 1 tsp **sugar** (optional, adjust to taste)

##### Garnishes:

* Finely chopped **scallions**
* Grated **daikon radish**
* **Wasabi** (fresh or paste)
* **Nori** (toasted seaweed), cut into thin strips
* Optional: toasted sesame seeds, sliced myoga (Japanese ginger), or even a soft-boiled egg on the side

### üî™ Preparation Steps

#### 1. **Make the Mentsuyu (dipping sauce):**

1. In a small saucepan, combine **dashi**, **soy sauce**, **mirin**, and **sugar**.
2. Bring to a gentle boil, then reduce and simmer for 2‚Äì3 minutes.
3. Let it cool to room temp, then chill in the fridge for at least 30 minutes. (Can be made ahead!)

> Pro tip: For extra depth, steep a piece of kombu or a dried shiitake mushroom in the mentsuyu overnight.

---

#### 2. **Cook the soba noodles:**

1. Bring a large pot of water to a rolling boil ‚Äî **no salt needed**.
2. Add the soba noodles, stir gently to separate, and cook according to package (usually 5‚Äì7 minutes).
3. Drain and **immediately rinse** under cold running water, rubbing the noodles gently with your hands to remove starch.
4. Once cool, **plunge into an ice water bath** for a few minutes to chill completely.
5. Drain and set aside on a **zaru** (bamboo mat) or a plate. Sprinkle with shredded **nori** if desired.

---

#### 3. **Assemble & Serve:**

1. Portion noodles onto serving plates.
2. Pour chilled mentsuyu into individual dipping bowls.
3. Serve garnishes on the side ‚Äî each diner customizes their dip to taste.
